## 8. AI AND AUTOMATED DATA PROCESSING

### Automated Systems Used

BFlow uses artificial intelligence and automated systems to enhance your experience:

- **AWS Textract**: Automatically extracts text from receipt and invoice photos you upload. This helps categorize and organize transactions.
- **AWS Rekognition**: Analyzes images for inappropriate content (NSFW detection) to maintain platform safety.
- **Automated Categorization**: Machine learning algorithms automatically categorize transactions based on merchant data and transaction descriptions.
- **Predictive Analytics**: Behavior-based analytics to provide spending insights and financial trends on your data.

### Accuracy and Responsibility

**Important:** Automated systems may produce errors. While we strive for accuracy, you are solely responsible for:
- Reviewing all automatically categorized transactions
- Verifying expense classifications
- Correcting any misclassifications in your account
- Auditing your financial data for accuracy

BFlow is a tracking tool only and is not intended to provide financial, investment, tax, or legal advice. Always independently verify important financial information.

### Data Usage for AI Improvement

We use aggregated, anonymized transaction data to improve our AI models and features. Your personal financial details are never shared with third parties for model training without your explicit consent.

### Transparency and Code Access

BFlow values transparency. Our backend code is open-source and publicly available on GitHub at [https://github.com/DiazzzDev/BFlow-Backend-Open-Source](https://github.com/DiazzzDev/BFlow-Backend-Open-Source).
